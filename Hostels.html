1.Paradise Hostels

Paradise Hostels are located 2km from the main campus and is the biggest private hostel. Paradise as the name suggests is the most peaceful hostel and a great place to interact with other comrades since it accomodates many students. Being a peaceful hostel, it provides a conducive environment for private studying.

Students can buy goods, groceries or eat in local restaurants in a small market 500m from the hostels.Hostel rent is Kshs.7,000/= or Kshs.8,000/= (per semester) for one person and Kshs.14,000/= or Kshs.16,000/= for two people(per semester). 2.Karen Hostel

Karen Hostel is located near a local market, 1.5km from the main campus. It is a classic, one-storey hostel having a total of 64 rooms. The rent is Kshs.9,000/= for 1 tenant per semester or Kshs.18,000/= for 2 tenants per semester. There is a wi-fi router(15mbps) serving each comrade in the hostel, but for those who have fully paid their rent. Its location near a market makes it cheap to buy goods or groceries from different shops and grocery stores. One can also eat in the available restaurants. Another advantage attached to its locality is that one can access matatus & motorbikes going to and from Rongo town. The landlord is Landlord 1 while the caretaker is Caretaker 3 RUC/000/2020 For more info., call Landlord 2: 0700000003

3.Oxford Hostel

Oxford Hostel is one of the luxurious private hostels. It is 2.5km far from the main campus. It is a four-storey building and has solar panels to provide electricity incase of power shortages or blackouts. There is also a 15mbps wi-fi connection there no need to buy internet bundles everytime. The rent is Kshs.18,000/= per semester for an individual or Kshs.36,000/= per semester for 2 tenants. The landlady is Landlady 1 while the caretaker is Caretaker 2 RUC/000/2020. For more info., contact Landlady 1:0700123456 or Caretaker 20700000002


4.Bethel Hostel

Bethel Hostel is one of the peaceful hostel. It is 2km far from the main campus. It is a four storey building and has a total of 50 rooms. Rent is Kshs. 10,000 per semester for an individual or Kshs.20,000/= for two individuals. Students can buy goods or groceries or eat in one of the local restaurants from the nearby market. The landlady is Landlady 2 while the caretaker is Caretaker 4 RUC/000/0000. For more info., contact Landlady 2:0712345678 or Caretaker 4:0700000004


Each hostel has at least 1 10,000litres water tank and a borehole hostel. Rain water supplements stored water thus there is adequate water for each comrade.
